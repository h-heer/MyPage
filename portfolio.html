Uber NYC Visulaization

  • Our project focused on analyzing Uber's ride data in New York City, utilizing R with ggplot2 and Tableau for visualization. Through rigorous data cleaning and preprocessing, we employed ggplot2 to create diverse visualizations like heatmaps and bar graphs, uncovering insights into ride frequency, duration, and revenue across different times and locations. Additionally, Tableau facilitated interactive dashboards, allowing stakeholders to explore data in depth.

  • Our analysis yielded key insights into busy routes, peak ride hours, and profitable locations, empowering Uber to optimize driver deployment and enhance service reliability during high-demand periods. Moreover, identifying profitable timeframes enabled targeted marketing efforts, enhancing customer acquisition and retention strategies. Leveraging advanced analytical tools, our project showcased the power of data-driven decision-making, providing Uber with actionable intelligence to navigate and excel in the competitive ride-sharing market of New York City.

AWS Security Notification System

  • In this project, our team created a security system leveraging AWS to enhance organizational security. The system was designed to notify the organization administrator immediately upon triggering any security alert. We developed a website using the Django framework, featuring a login page for secure access.

  • The system flagged security events by scanning user activity logs stored in Amazon S3 buckets. An EC2 instance was used to monitor these logs for suspicious activity or predefined security events. When such an event was detected, the system automatically sent notifications to the administrator via email or SMS, enabling rapid response and mitigation.

Bike Store Website

  • As the project manager, I led a team in planning the creation of a Shopify store for a bike shop struggling to adapt to the restrictions of COVID-19. Our objective was to transition their business online to maintain sales and customer engagement during the pandemic. We designed an intuitive and user-friendly e-commerce platform that would showcase the shop's product range, facilitate online purchases, and integrate inventory management. The plan also included a feature for customers to book appointment times for video calls with biking sales professionals, providing personalized assistance remotely.

  • The project scope included setting up secure payment gateways, optimizing the site for search engines, and providing training for the bike shop staff to manage the online store. Although the project ultimately did not come to fruition, the planning process provided valuable insights and experience in managing e-commerce solutions and remote team collaboration.
